---

## 🧭 Purpose

This sprint is designed to answer one question:

> **Where does the governance → operations gap actually exist inside the system?**

Not in theory.
Not in architecture diagrams.

In **live workflows and real decisions**.

---

## ⚙️ Sprint Structure

![Structure](https://img.shields.io/badge/structure-4_week_model-C0890A?style=for-the-badge)

### Week 1 — System Mapping

![Week1](https://img.shields.io/badge/week_1-diagnose-8E9FD5?style=flat-square)

* Map governance → execution pathways
* Identify ownership gaps
* Surface decision bottlenecks

---

### Week 2 — Friction Identification

![Week2](https://img.shields.io/badge/week_2-friction_analysis-8E9FD5?style=flat-square)

* Analyze workflow-level breakdowns
* Identify where AI fails to translate into action
* Map trust and adoption barriers

---

### Week 3 — Operator Layer Design

![Week3](https://img.shields.io/badge/week_3-design-8E9FD5?style=flat-square)

* Define “operations-sponsored governance” model
* Embed decision ownership into workflows
* Design feedback loop enforcement mechanisms

---

### Week 4 — Validation

![Week4](https://img.shields.io/badge/week_4-validate-8E9FD5?style=flat-square)

* Test model against real scenarios
* Validate improvements in decision velocity
* Identify rollout pathways

---

## 🧪 Sprint Characteristics

![Characteristics](https://img.shields.io/badge/approach-operator_first-2D3436?style=flat-square)

* Focused, not open-ended
* Diagnostic, not disruptive
* Embedded, not external
* Execution-aware, not theoretical

---

## 🎯 Core Principle

![Principle](https://img.shields.io/badge/principle-reality_over_theory-2D8A6B?style=for-the-badge)

> The system must be evaluated where it actually runs:

**inside operations**

---

## 📌 Closing Thought

![Closing](https://img.shields.io/badge/frame-clarity_before_scale-E24B4A?style=for-the-badge)

Before scaling AI further:

> Ensure the system can **execute reliably at the operator level**
