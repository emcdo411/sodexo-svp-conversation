### 🧱 Failure Stack

![Ownership](https://img.shields.io/badge/Ownership-missing_operator_owner-C0890A?style=flat-square)
![Drift](https://img.shields.io/badge/Criteria_Drift-undetected-C0890A?style=flat-square)

![Latency](https://img.shields.io/badge/Decision_Latency-increasing-C0890A?style=flat-square)

1. **Governance has no operator owner**
   → Responsibility exists structurally, not operationally

2. **Criteria drift is invisible**
   → Systems operate on outdated assumptions without signaling

3. **Feedback loops are not enforced**
   → Human corrections never become system intelligence

4. **Decision latency increases over time**
   → Friction compounds, velocity declines
