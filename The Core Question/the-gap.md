## 🧩 Working Hypothesis

![Ownership](https://img.shields.io/badge/ownership-fractured-6b7280?style=flat-square)
![Structure](https://img.shields.io/badge/structure-misaligned-6b7280?style=flat-square)
![Execution](https://img.shields.io/badge/execution-distributed-6b7280?style=flat-square)

The gap exists because the system is split across incompatible ownership models:

* **Governance** → centralized (policy, compliance, architecture)
* **Execution** → distributed (operators, field teams, workflows)
* **Bridge Layer** → *undefined*
