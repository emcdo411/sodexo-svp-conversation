---

## 🧭 Purpose

This model defines what it actually means for:

> **Governance to be sponsored through operations**

Not conceptually.
Not philosophically.

But as a **working system inside real execution environments**.

---

## 🔥 Core Shift

![Shift](https://img.shields.io/badge/shift-structural_not_incremental-A32D2D?style=for-the-badge)

Traditional governance assumes:

```text id="oldmodel1"
Governance → Policy → Enforcement → Operations
```

The operations sponsorship model reverses this:

```text id="newmodel1"
Operations → Workflow → Governance → Continuous Adjustment
```

This is not an improvement.

It is a **reallocation of system authority**.

---

## 🧩 Model Definition

![Definition](https://img.shields.io/badge/model-operations_sponsored-2D8A6B?style=flat-square)

Governance is no longer a standalone function.

It becomes a **byproduct of operational execution**, defined by:

* Real decisions
* Real constraints
* Real outcomes

---

## 🧱 Core Components

![Components](https://img.shields.io/badge/components-required_layers-C0890A?style=for-the-badge)

### 1. Embedded Decision Ownership

![Ownership](https://img.shields.io/badge/ownership-explicit-8E9FD5?style=flat-square)

* Every AI-assisted decision has a **named operator owner**
* Ownership is tied to execution, not oversight
* Accountability is visible and enforceable

---

### 2. Workflow-Level Integration

![Workflow](https://img.shields.io/badge/integration-native_to_workflow-8E9FD5?style=flat-square)

* AI is embedded directly into operational systems
* No external dashboards required for action
* Decisions occur **inside the workflow**, not alongside it

---

### 3. Feedback Loop Enforcement

![Feedback](https://img.shields.io/badge/feedback-enforced_not_optional-8E9FD5?style=flat-square)

* Every override or correction is captured
* Human judgment becomes system intelligence
* Feedback is structured, not anecdotal

---

### 4. Criteria Validity Monitoring

![Criteria](https://img.shields.io/badge/criteria-dynamic_validity-8E9FD5?style=flat-square)

* Decision logic is continuously evaluated
* Drift is detected before it impacts outcomes
* Governance adapts to changing conditions

---

### 5. Trust Calibration Layer

![Trust](https://img.shields.io/badge/trust-calibrated-8E9FD5?style=flat-square)

* Clear boundaries for when AI should be trusted
* Clear signals for when human override is required
* Confidence is **designed**, not assumed

---
