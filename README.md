## 🔥 Core Tension

> **Governance without operational sponsorship becomes theater.**
> **Deployment without governance becomes drift.**

These are not equal positions.

They represent **two incomplete systems**:

* **Sodexo** → structurally sound, operationally constrained
* **Compass** → operationally aggressive, structurally exposed

---

## 🧩 The Hypothesis

The gap exists because:

![Ownership](https://img.shields.io/badge/ownership-fractured-6b7280?style=flat-square)
![Incentives](https://img.shields.io/badge/incentives-misaligned-6b7280?style=flat-square)
![Feedback](https://img.shields.io/badge/feedback-loops_missing-6b7280?style=flat-square)
![Trust](https://img.shields.io/badge/trust-inconsistent-6b7280?style=flat-square)

* Governance is centralized
* Execution is distributed
* Ownership of the bridge is undefined

---

## 🧠 Operator Insight (Ground Truth)

> “Closing the gap in governance must be sponsored through operations.”

### Implication:

If operations is the sponsor, then operations must own:

* Decision accountability
* Feedback loops
* Criteria validity
* Trust calibration

---

## 🧪 Where Systems Break

![Failure](https://img.shields.io/badge/failure-mode-systemic-A32D2D?style=flat-square)

1. Governance has **no embedded operator owner**
2. AI outputs lack **workflow integration**
3. Criteria drift goes **undetected**
4. Decision latency increases over time

### Result:

AI becomes:

```text
Measured → Reported → Audited
```

But never:

```text
Trusted → Embedded → Relied upon
```
